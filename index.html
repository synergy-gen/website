About

Synergen (short for “Synergy Generator”) is not your typical goal tracking app. It creates an environment where users can thrive off the collective energy of others as they accomplish their goals. Not only can users create goals, but they can also adopt goals shared by others as their own and join groups of other users working towards the same goal. This ability to search through the productive creations of users and participate in them sets Synergen apart. With the power of a social network to facilitate goal-setting and goal-getting, your dreams are now within reach.

Motivation

Existing mobile applications for goal tracking focus on the individual - there is no network associated with the goals you create. This leaves everyone with a mindset of “you’re on your own” when it comes to setting and accomplishing their goals. Shouldn’t there be a way for users to create, share, and adopt goals in a wonderfully simple and beautiful way? Welcome Synergen, bringing you together so that nothing is impossible.
